About MAYA Consultancy

Our Vision

To be the guiding light that empowers businesses across Ethiopia and beyond, fostering financial literacy, operational excellence, and sustainable growth practices that contribute to economic development and prosperity.

Our Mission

To inspire growth, drive excellence, and create sustainable success for every business we serve through innovative financial strategies, personalized consulting solutions, and unwavering commitment to our clients' vision and goals.

Our Core Values

These principles guide our approach to every client engagement and shape our organizational culture.

Integrity & Ethics

We uphold the highest standards of honesty, transparency, and ethical conduct in all our client relationships and business practices.

Client-Centric Approach

We place our clients' needs at the center of everything we do, tailoring our solutions to address their unique challenges and objectives.

Adaptability

We embrace change and continuously evolve our approaches to meet the dynamic needs of our clients in a rapidly changing business environment.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in all our services, consistently delivering high-quality, impactful solutions that exceed client expectations.

Inclusivity & Diversity

We value diverse perspectives and foster an inclusive environment that respects and celebrates differences in thought, background, and experience.

Local Expertise

We combine global best practices with deep understanding of the Ethiopian business landscape to provide contextually relevant solutions.
